Medications to Avoid During Pregnancy

These should NOT be taken unless specifically prescribed by a doctor due to risk to the baby:

  • Ibuprofen, Aspirin, Naproxen (pain relievers)
  • Certain antibiotics like Tetracycline
  • Some anti-acne drugs (e.g., Isotretinoin)
  • Herbal supplements with unknown safety

General Guidelines
  1. Always take prenatal vitamins daily, ideally at the same time each day.
  2. Stay hydrated – at least 8–10 glasses of water per day.
  3. Avoid alcohol, smoking, and caffeine.
  4. Consult OB-GYN before starting any new medication.
  5. Keep a record of all medications taken during pregnancy for reference during prenatal check-ups.
